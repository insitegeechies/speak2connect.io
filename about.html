The Team

Who are we

At our core, we are a team dedicated to delivering top-notch service in a variety of domains. As experts in the realm of REVA (Real Estate Virtual Assistant)/Telemarketing, Appointment Setting, Cold Calling, Lead Generation, Telemarketing, Admin Work, Website and Web-based App Development, and Virtual Assistance, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence.

While our team was only established in 2018, every member of our team had been handling calls for years, and we can say that we are used to talking to people and building rapport. We are go-getters, we focus more on the solutions than pointing fingers and getting stuck with a problem. We make sure that we produce quality calls.

We are efficient in skip and list tracing using Popstream and spreadsheets. We can submit up to 400 leads in a day. We are trainable, submit tasks with quality, and we assure you that we work with integrity.

We offer a variety of services that would help our client's company grow, such as:

  • Cold Calling
  • Warm Calling
  • Lead Nurturing
  • List Tracing
  • Chat Support
  • Email Support
  • Email handling
  • Quality Assurance Analyst

We highly value professional commitment and the client's reputation. We always make sure to go the extra mile to provide high-quality service on every task the client has assigned to me. We work with integrity and with little to no supervision needed.
